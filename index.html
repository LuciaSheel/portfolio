Bio

A bit about my background and education...I have 35 years experience in the service industry. My last day was March 16, 2020, when the world closed down due to the COVID-19 pandemic. I was invited back when things opened up, but I decided to retire and work on my education. I have since obtained a bookkeeping certificate and a virtual assistant certificate from Okanagan College. I was looking for my next educational challenge when I came across the Circuit Stream software development bootcamp. It seemed like a good fit, so I enrolled and I am currently learning many new and exciting things!
